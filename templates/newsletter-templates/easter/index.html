DIY Natural Egg Dyes

Add a teaspoon of vinegar to 2 cups of water and one of the following:

  • 2 cups raw beets
  • 2 cups crushed blueberries
  • 1 tsp of tumeric

Boil for 10 minutes. Allow to cool and dip your hard boiled eggs in the dye, turning often for an even dye.
